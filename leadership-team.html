Our Team

Balram Kisan

Co-founder

Balram Kisan

The founder of the B Kisan venture that is doing wonders around the world, Balram Kisan is a one-man army in the field of agro science and farming. He has to his credit innumerable ventures and initiatives for the upliftment of farmers in the rural areas with a primary focus on collective involvement leading to enhanced crop production, sustainable & smart farming, dedicated research & development in the allied fields and also unique trading initiatives impacting not just the local or regional community but also leading to global presence.

Sh Balram Kisan has enabled himself well through various formal and informal agro education qualifications. And he has utilised these abundantly to put into practice a large number of projects with the involvement of local communities, multinational vendors, NGOs and govt agencies, both domestically and at international leval.

Recognized variously for his contributions both by govt and non govt organisations his most honorable achievement is the re-christening of his name from Balram Jat to Balram Kisan by the millions of people connected with him.

The driving force behind the Oxygen Purodhyan Project nurtured through his almost two decades of experience in the agronomics, agriculture and associated training, production, trading, research & development and sustainable and smart farming initiatives.
All the ventures, initiatives, research work and associated projects undertaken by B Kisan have an involvement and supervision of Balram Kisan. With an acute eye for understanding agro related issues, their solutions and visionary initiatives he is the pillar around which the team will rally to make this outstanding project a reality.

Chiragbhai Ratanjibhai
Sanja
Chiragbhai Ratanjibhai Sanja

A young entrepreneur with expertise in inner & outer well-being and development, sustainable & smart farming, he is the Co-founder of the Oxygen Purodhyan Project. Currently associated with multiple ongoing initiatives of the B Kisan he will provide focus on sustainable farming and external liaison and take ahead the Industrial Park aspects as per the collective vision of the project.

At thirty-eight years, Chiragbhai has immense experience in indigenous wellness, rural development and sustainable & smart farming apart from a generous indulgence in the manufacturing sector. Chiragbhai is one of the two Co-founders of the Oxygen Purodhyan Venture.

Chiragbhai is partnering with us with a focus on spiritual and sustainable farming aspects of Oxygen Purodhyan Project. Chief amongst his supervisions is the following

  • The Humaniversity Guidelines of spiritual, inner & outer well-being journey
  • Sajeevan Foundation Works on gram vikas and seed to packing food cycle
  • Kisan Pathshala Sustainable and smart farming with specialization in bamboo & banana farming at low cost and high yield

Anjali Iyer Venkatesh
Anjali Iyer Venkatesh

The second Co-founder of the project, Wg Cdr Anjali Iyer is a dynamic, high performing, richly awarded retired Indian Air Force Officer. She brings with her almost twenty five years of aviation combat, administrative and logistics leadership. Highly adept at translating business strategies and objectives into HR initiatives. With her being qualified at both Graduate and Post Graduate levels in Environmental Sciences she is naturally suited as one of the main pillars of Oxygen Purodhyan.

Second Co-founder of the Oxygen Purodhyan Project Wing Commander Anjali Iyer has more than two decades of experience with the Indian Air Force and subsequently with Hindustan Zinc Limited, Vedanta.

An accomplished management leader, richly awarded during her stint with the IAF, with a competitive track record of translating business strategies & objectives into practices. Highly proficient in spearheading HR functions to drive impact-driven talent management, build high performing teams for supporting company vision and directing facility management projects. Adept at liaising with govt institutions and C-level executives to manage transformation projects with in-depth understanding of policies and industry goals.
