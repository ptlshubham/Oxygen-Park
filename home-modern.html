Our Goal is To Provide Healthy Food and Supplimentry direct through farm to plate (farm to home).
A trident initiative with Plantation & Forestry, Retreat & Wellness, and Industrial Park. A Self Sustainable Environment & Economy (S2E2) where all components shall be modeled as Self or Help Programmes. Oxygen Purodhyan is aimed to be self-sustaining, and eco-friendly and is expected to contribute greatly to the preservation of the ecosystem and native Indian concepts of well-being.
