Why Are We Qualified For This Project?

Decades of Agri Innovations

BKisan has toiled for over three decades in the field of agri innovations starting the journey from grassroots levels. We acquired knowhow and innovated on it as our experience and domain specialization expanded and firmed up. We have been part of agri initiatives pan India and have worked almost in the entire country and abroad affecting, alleviating and elevating more than a few crore farmers in this journey.

Our next phase is planned to be almost revolutionary with a major emphasis on Indianness, Sustainable Environment and Economy.

Dynamic and Expert Core Group

Spearheaded by Balram Kisan who has a proven expertise in the field of agronomics, research & development, training, marketing and human connect, along with the two co-founders, Chiragbhai Sanja and Wg Cdr Anjali Iyer (Retd) form a formidable core group ideally suited for this project.

The Core Group provides the most ideal mix of leadership to drive this venture with their experience, knowledge, foresight and industrious approach, taking the collective vision to fruition in an efficient and effective manner.

The core group has varied expertise in farming, agro research & development, agro trading, export ventures, sustainable & smart farming, wellness and self-growth, ayurvedic & related wellness apart from collective business development and human resource management skills.

Certifications & Recognitions

The B Kisan venture has a number of recognitions and certifications to speak for its efforts and results. Also, the proven impact of these could be gauged clearly by the outcomes amongst various farmers, traders, researchers and multinational vendors. With a following of more than ten lakh people from various fields directly or indirectly associated with us, we provide the ideal launch pad for this visionary project.
