National Bamboo Mission

As Balram Kisan and RFC established supremacy in the rapid development in the field of input for agriculture, in 2016 RFC became a member of the National Bamboo Mission. This was a natural extension of the vision of Sh Balram Kisan. The RFC now produced varieties of bamboo saplings, creating tissue & culture of bamboos and supplying the same pan India. B Kisan became the first and probably the only private player supplying bamboo to the Govt of India wherein saplings were being supplied to Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh and most recently to Gujarat and Uttar Pradesh.

The contribution to this Mission has been far reaching viz lowering of global temperatures, increase in oxygen levels, rain water harvesting projects, enhancing carbon ratings and very importantly decreasing the imports while providing an impetus to bamboo exports.
