How We Works!!

Our Major Initiative

Early Ventures

With extremely humble beginnings in Barwani, a small town three and a half hours drive from Indore in Madhya Pradesh, Sh Balram Jat after passing Std Tenth pursued his yearning for being an independent organizer and promoter. A diploma in Electronics from ITI Indore in 1997 followed, after which delved into helping farmers with Drip Line Mapping, Motors & Pumps installations etc. In 2006, along with sixteen friends from diverse backgrounds, some being doctors, engineers, farmers et al he commenced his eventful journey in agriculture and started the KISAN PATHSHALA; the first but most significant step in the voyage of B Kisan. What followed is nothing short of an agriculture revolution.

Kisan Pathshala-Learn & Teach

Eight years after the initial small steps B Kisan started its first venture, the Kisan Pathshala. The unique perceived benefit envisaged from this was Kharcha Aadha Utpaadan Zyada through a concept of Learn & Teach. Sixteen of his associates collaborated on this in person meetings, trainings, seminars in order to learn and teach agro aspects. Leading agronomists were invited to the maiden workshop at Barwani attended by almost an army of local farmers. Aimed at development of farming in the area and educating farmers pan India, Kisan Pathshala came to be known for its popularity and effectiveness in delivery. Learning from the experience of past five years, Kisan Pathshala underwent its first metamorphosis. Multiple channels were unified under a single window providing a portal, email interaction, tele-calling and a YouTube channel.

Kisan Darbar

With the vision of comprehensive development of the farmers as envisioned by Balram Kisan a missing cog in the wheel being developed by B Kisan was found to be an active and readily available farmers’ support. Thus the Kisan Darbar was setup and continues to this day actively providing agro support to the farmers.

YouTube Channel

The YouTube channel - किसान पाठशाला BALRAM KISAN - was launched in 2012. However due to restricted availability of internet facilities this could go fully live only by 2019. This swiftly became a vital platform for sharing education and knowledge in the agro fields. Bamboo, papaya, banana, sugarcane etc found prominence amongst other crops on this platform.

Simplify Agriculture Education

This was launched as part of YouTube B Kisan initiative to impart knowledge and training in simple succulent manner to the farmers at a very rudimentary level. Briskly the channel surpassed one lakh subscribers with more than a one crore twenty lakh people connected with it. The first seminar organized under the aegis of Simplify Agriculture Education had more than three lakh people interacting on a one-on-one basis.

Outreach Program

Between 2006 and 2021 B Kisan utilized the Kisan Pathshala platform to put in place an outreach programme taking agro education to localities where needed. If more than twentyfive farmers requisitioned, B Kisan reached out to provide all assistance at no cost.

Agro Research & Development

Reva Flora Culture (RFC)

Having established the basic necessities in the agro environment for the farmers including education, training, production, marketing and agro support the acquired expertise was decided to be put to better use to further boost the agro cycle. In 2014, The Reva Flora Culture was conceived by Balram Kisan and six farmers carefully selected as the core group from amongst the pool of Kisan Pathshala constituents. Spread over 4 acres of land in Borlay, Barwani, MP with a capacity of 25 lakh saplings per year the production commenced in 2015 and by 2016, in less than a year the supply of these started to the farmers. Today the annual capacity of RFC is three crore culture production.

RFC Laboratory

As part of the Reva Flora Culture (RFC) the RFC Laboratory was set up. Starting with six-cycle disease resistant saplings the RFC developed a four-cycle saplings and increased the production dramatically to three crore saplings a year with the RFC now spread over 18 acres of land. The Reva G-9 is the flagship product now being exported to various countries. Micropropagation of bamboo saplings is another outcome which enables even small farmers to create tissue and culture bamboo saplings at home. The training is being provided by B Kisan.

Our Collective Ventures

BKisan Traders

(Agro Marketing of Farm to Plate by BK Banana)

With rapid progress in the farming as a result of Kisan Pathshala the produce became abundant and a need was felt to market the same. Thus, in 2010, came into being Sant Kripa, a Farmers Producer Organisation (FPO), spearheaded by Balram Kisan with twenty-one farmers as members. This was later re-modelled and christened as B Kisan Traders, the first venture of B Kisan with a collective business outlook for the community. This has majorly resulted into marketing the local produce of Barwani not only regionally but across the country. Banana, tomatoes, ladyfingers, cucumber etc were the major produce which found buyers across the country in overwhelming numbers.

BKisan Smart Farming Pvt Ltd

(Agro Marketing)

This was setup in 2019 to promote trading for the farmers at a sizeable scale. It provides a trading platform to a considerable number of local farmers for trading in their commodities. A unique product – vysinOMeta was introduced by BKisan as part of this initiative, available only on this platform and nowhere else in the international market.

BKisan Farmer Producer Co Ltd

(Agri Input/Agri Output & Soil Testing Pan India)

Set up in 2021 it’s a most ambitious project of BKisan so far with far reaching outcomes for the environment and enormous payoffs for the common farmers. The largest such setup in the country so far, apart from the considerable gains for the farmers, it aims at a great positive impact on the climate & the environment by addressing the carbon emissions, hill forestry, table forestry and other agro input and output for the farmers.

70:30 Farming

(Farming Model for Residue Free Farming)

Gaining from the expertise acquired, practices learnt and collective understanding through Kisan Pathshala this initiative was launched on about one thousand acres of land in 2012. This was aimed at reducing the input expenditure by half, substantially reduce fertilizer usage and ensuring premium results with enhanced production. Test trials were carried out in MP, Gujrat & Maharashtra with different types of land, farmers & crops. This is a revolutionary step towards simplifying agro techniques and obtaining residue free, healthy output with increased income – Kisan Kharcha Aadha Utpaadan Zyada. This scheme provided everything free of cost to the farmers.

Development of Barwani & Regional Impact

As a result of the constant development of the region tomatoes were being supplied to the entire north India, banana to the entire country and exported through Reliance, Mother Dairy and Mahindra.

Vendor Trading with Multinationals

While trading through various multinationals as brought out above the catchment areas of B Kisan were expanded to include regions of Burhanpur, Jalgaon, Raver, Rajpipla etc. Desai Agri Foods and Dharti Exports established their hubs at Barwani giving significant boost to the local produce. Banana, Chilies, Ladyfingers from the region are now being exported around the world while a sizeable share of the domestic market for tomatoes is being met through efforts of B Kisan. BK Banana, a composite of BKisan along with ties up with these vendors aimed to increase banana exports from a mere 3% to a massive 30%. Since 2006 a figure of 6.3% has already been achieved.

BKisan (Guj) Farmers Producer Company Ltd

A Farmers Produce Organisation (FPO) started in 2021 with a focus on Gujarat on similar lines as BKisan.
