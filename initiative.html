Our Major Initiative

Early Ventures

With extremely humble beginnings in Barwani, a small town three and a half hours drive from Indore in Madhya Pradesh, Sh Balram Jat after passing Std Tenth pursued his yearning for being an independent organizer and promoter. A diploma in Electronics from ITI Indore in 1997 followed, after which delved into helping farmers with Drip Line Mapping, Motors & Pumps installations etc. In 2006, along with sixteen friends from diverse backgrounds, some being doctors, engineers, farmers et al he commenced his eventful journey in agriculture and started the KISAN PATHSHALA; the first but most significant step in the voyage of B Kisan. What followed is nothing short of an agriculture revolution.

Kisan Pathshala-Learn & Teach

Eight years after the initial small steps B Kisan started its first venture, the Kisan Pathshala. The unique perceived benefit envisaged from this was Kharcha Aadha Utpaadan Zyada through a concept of Learn & Teach. Sixteen of his associates collaborated on this in person meetings, trainings, seminars in order to learn and teach agro aspects. Leading agronomists were invited to the maiden workshop at Barwani attended by almost an army of local farmers. Aimed at development of farming in the area and educating farmers pan India, Kisan Pathshala came to be known for its popularity and effectiveness in delivery. Learning from the experience of past five years, Kisan Pathshala underwent its first metamorphosis. Multiple channels were unified under a single window providing a portal, email interaction, tele-calling and a YouTube channel.

Kisan Darbar

With the vision of comprehensive development of the farmers as envisioned by Balram Kisan a missing cog in the wheel being developed by B Kisan was found to be an active and readily available farmers’ support. Thus the Kisan Darbar was setup and continues to this day actively providing agro support to the farmers.

YouTube Channel

The YouTube channel - किसान पाठशाला BALRAM KISAN - was launched in 2012. However due to restricted availability of internet facilities this could go fully live only by 2019. This swiftly became a vital platform for sharing education and knowledge in the agro fields. Bamboo, papaya, banana, sugarcane etc found prominence amongst other crops on this platform.

Simplify Agriculture Education

This was launched as part of YouTube B Kisan initiative to impart knowledge and training in simple succulent manner to the farmers at a very rudimentary level. Briskly the channel surpassed one lakh subscribers with more than a one crore twenty lakh people connected with it. The first seminar organized under the aegis of Simplify Agriculture Education had more than three lakh people interacting on a one-on-one basis.

Outreach Program

Between 2006 and 2021 B Kisan utilized the Kisan Pathshala platform to put in place an outreach programme taking agro education to localities where needed. If more than twentyfive farmers requisitioned, B Kisan reached out to provide all assistance at no cost.
